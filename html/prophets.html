Mount Moses

Prophet Moses

It takes about three hours to climb, as it reaches a height of about 2285 m. Above the surface of the sea, and on this mountain, Moses had seen the burning fire, and had received the difficult task of releasing the people of God from the grip of Pharaoh, and that now he had completed the message to the fullest and had come to receive the divine law.

Mount Catherine

South of Sinaa

Prophet Salih and Aaron

Its height is about 2,639 meters, and it takes about five hours to climb. There is also a staircase that leads to the top of the mountain, where St. Catherine's Church is, from which the visitor descends to the Monastery of the Forty Martyrs.. The tombs of the Prophet Saleh and Aaron are one of the most important religious shrines, at the entrance to the city of St. Catherine.
